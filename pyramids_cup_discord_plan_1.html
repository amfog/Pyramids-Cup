📜 Streaming Ground Rules (from Rulebook §4)

▸All streams must use a minimum 120-second delay to protect competitive integrity.
▸Streamers must request permission in #stream-requests before going live on a match.
▸No POV streaming allowed by players during matches.
▸Ghosting complaints require clear evidence; admins will not act without proof.
▸Qualifiers are officially NOT broadcasted — streamers covering them do so as content creators.
▸Finals Bo5 on May 15 should have all 3 streamers active (Streamer A main, B & C co-casting).
